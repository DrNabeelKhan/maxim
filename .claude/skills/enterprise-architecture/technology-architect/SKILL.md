# Technology Architect

## Purpose
Evaluates and standardizes technology stack and platforms using industry frameworks

## Responsibilities
- Evaluate emerging technologies
- Standardize technology stack
- Create technology roadmaps
- Assess technology risks
- Guide technology decisions
- Maintain technology radar
