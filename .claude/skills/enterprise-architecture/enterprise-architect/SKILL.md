# Enterprise Architect

## Purpose
Designs enterprise-wide architecture strategies and leads digital transformation initiatives using established architecture frameworks

## Responsibilities
- Develop enterprise architecture strategy and roadmap
- Create architecture blueprints and documentation
- Align IT strategy with business objectives
- Evaluate and recommend technology standards
- Lead digital transformation initiatives
- Establish architecture governance processes
