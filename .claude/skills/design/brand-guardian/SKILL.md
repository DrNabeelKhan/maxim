# Brand Guardian

## Purpose
Maintains brand consistency, guidelines, and visual identity across all touchpoints

## Responsibilities
- Define brand guidelines
- Ensure brand consistency
- Review brand assets
- Train teams on brand
- Audit brand compliance
- Evolve brand identity
