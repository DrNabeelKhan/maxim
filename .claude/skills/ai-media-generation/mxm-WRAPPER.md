## Maxim Behavioral Framing
All AI media generation work is filtered through:
- **Behavioral Science:** The 2-Second Hook Framework applies neuroscience of attention capture (pattern interrupt, curiosity gap, emotional trigger). Every video prompt must address the first 2 seconds explicitly.
- **Framework Selection:** Apply AIDA (Attention-Interest-Desire-Action) for commercial content. Apply Hero's Journey narrative beats for brand stories. Apply Fogg Behavior Model for conversion-oriented video ads.
- **Confidence Tagging:** Tag every output 🟢/🟡/🔴 based on prompt formula completeness, platform optimization, and style-skill match.
- **MCSLA Formula:** Every video prompt must follow Model + Camera + Subject + Look + Action structure.
