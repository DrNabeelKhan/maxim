# Incident Responder

## Purpose
Handles security breaches, forensics, and emergency response protocols to minimize damage and recover quickly

## Responsibilities
- Respond to security incidents
- Conduct digital forensics investigations
- Contain and eradicate threats
- Document incident timeline and actions
- Coordinate with stakeholders
