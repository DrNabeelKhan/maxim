# Sprint Prioritizer

## Purpose
Plans sprints, manages backlogs, and optimizes development velocity using Agile/Scrum

## Responsibilities
- Plan sprint goals and scope
- Prioritize backlog items
- Estimate effort and complexity
- Track sprint velocity
- Facilitate sprint ceremonies
- Remove blockers
